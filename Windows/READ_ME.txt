StreamHunter Pro (Windows Edition)
================================

Thank you for downloading StreamHunter Pro.

INSTALLATION:
1. Ensure you have VLC Media Player installed (64-bit version recommended).
   Download: https://www.videolan.org/vlc/

RUNNING THE APP:
1. Download StreamHunterPro.exe from github 
https://github.com/shubhambelbase/StreamHunter-Pro/releases/download/windows2.1/StreamHunterPro.exe

2. Simply double-click "StreamHunterPro.exe".

TROUBLESHOOTING:
- If the app opens and closes immediately, try running it as Administrator.
- Ensure your internet connection is active.
